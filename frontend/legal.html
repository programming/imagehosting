Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

We collect as little data as possible.

What we store

  • The image file you upload — held in Cloudflare R2 storage
  • Basic metadata: file type, file size, expiry time, and burn-after-reading flag
  • An anonymous daily upload counter used for rate limiting

We do not store your IP address, browser, device, or any personal information.

How long we keep it

  • All images are permanently deleted after 24 hours
  • Images with burn-after-reading enabled are deleted immediately on first view
  • No backups are kept

Third parties

This service runs on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Cloudflare may process request metadata (such as IP addresses) as part of routing and DDoS protection, subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy. We have no access to this data and do not share it with anyone.

No cookies are set. No analytics are collected. No third-party scripts are loaded.
